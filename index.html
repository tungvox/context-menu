How to Play Snake

  1. Pick up the controller with your right hand (right-click anywhere on the screen)
  2. You can drag the controller to a comfortable spot
  3. Press 'A' to start the game
  4. Use the D-pad to move the snake
  5. Eat the food (red apple) to grow longer
  6. Avoid hitting the walls, obstacles, or yourself
  7. Press 'B' to pause/resume the game
  8. Try to grow as long as possible without crashing!
